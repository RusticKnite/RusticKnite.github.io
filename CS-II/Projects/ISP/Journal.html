Development Engineer:

The development engineer's job is to oversee the software aspect of the project and to optimize its efficiency. They will work to optimize and improve the project to help keep up with the demands and goals of the project. This role would be more focused on the technical aspect of the project and will focus on more of a supervisor role. The characteristics required for this role is to be disciplined, hardworking, flexible, and responsible. Lots of the challenges that will be faced by this role is debugging which they must overcome in order to see the success of the project. This role can only be filled by someone that has vastly knowledgeable in the topic and takes a considerable amount of skill. Therefore, this role is very vital to our project and it is important to maintain this role within our group.

QA Engineer:

A quality assurance engineer is supposed to test the condition of the project and fix any problems that arise. They would fix problems such as bugs, errors, unexpected results, and poor quality. This role would constantly be running test and assure that the project is moving smooth at all times. This role would need to be attentive, detailed, critical, and efficient. A quality engineer is vital to a project because without them issues could go indicted for long periods of time setting the team back for even longer when the problem evolves into something worse. This role also helps keep the project close to its ideal goals and allows for other roles to work more efficiently.

Project Manager:

The project manager is responsible for keeping the team in a schedule and planning. This role acts like a supervisor for the rest of team and raises the efficiency by keeping everyone to a deadline. They are responsible for ensuring a reasonable deadline and making sure that the development of the project is running smoothly. This role has to be responsible, honest, commanding, and detailed in order to be executed properly. This role is also made to give aid and guidance to other roles to help keep the flow of the schedule. The project manager is key to creating a successful project because they form the foundation for which the group can build off of.

Product Manager:

The product manager is responsible for improving the product based off it's ability to reflect the goals of the group and the audience.They will also lead in the hardware aspect of the project and work to improve the product in everyday. This role needs to be innovative, creative, handy, and crafty in order to best fit this job. This role makes the connections between all the aspects that go into the project and improves hoses bonds by implementing them into the project. This role is vital for the project because it constantly shapes are project to become even better than our goals and allows us to grow in our ideas and design.

Release Engineer:

The release engineer's responsibility is to ensure the execution of the software and all the components related to it. This role would be responsible of the GitHub and repository of the project to ensure back ups, storage, organization, and completion. This job helps keep the work safe and organized while also testing it and making sure that everything is accessible. The engineer focuses on making sure that the code is in final form and ready. This role needs to be attentive, hardworking, efficient, organized, and responsible in order to be successful. This role is vital for team because it ensures that are technical aspect of the project is secure and helps it polish up the project as a whole.
